Q: What is a typical CoderGirl meet up like?

After weekly announcements, you may break into a mini-group according to your interests. Currently, we have mini-groups for Java, Web Development, SQL, and Harvard 50x problems sets. You can also just hang-out and work on your own project or collaborate with others. Each meet-up will have several experienced Mentors available to ask for guidance. Furthermore, every other week will feature a guest speaker to discuss various topics such as careers, skill-building, and self-care as it relates to coding.

Q: My school-aged daughter is interested in coding. Can I bring her?

Absolutely! It's well known that girls can become discouraged from pursing STEM fields like computer science. CoderGirl offers a supportive environment where she can feel comfortable exploring and learning code.

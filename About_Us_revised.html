LaunchCoderGirl is a LaunchCode community program that is an all-women's coding meet-up. CoderGirl provides a space for women of all skill levels to explore topics in programming and technology, and be supported by a community of like-minded individuals. This meet-up is an opportunity for women to network, learn, and create.

Come join us! Women of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels are welcome!
We meet every Wednesday @ 4240 Duncan Ave, St. Louis, MO 63110 on the 2nd floor
in the Cambridge Innovation Community (CIC) from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m.

We're on social media! Check us out on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram to stay updated on
CoderGirl Happenings!
